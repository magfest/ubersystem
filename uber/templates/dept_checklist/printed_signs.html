Room Signage for {{ department.name }}

Signage comes in three types: 12"x18" coroplast, 24"x36" coroplast, and 33"x80" retractable floor banner. All attendee-facing rooms will automatically receive either a 24"x36" coroplast or 33"x80" retractable sign for outside of the room.

Please submit requests for additional signage here, and be sure to specify FOR EACH SIGN:

  • Number needed
  • Size of sign
  • Primary text of sign
  • Sub text of sign (if applicable)
  • Intended placement location for sign
  • Link to any custom artwork that is needed
  • Any additional notes

Note: This checklist item is NOT for printed materials such as sign-up forms, waivers, or anything else that goes on standard office paper. STOPS has a paper cutter and a printer for small jobs (less than 50 pages). Anything more than 50 pages needs to go into the Bulk Print Jobs checklist item.
