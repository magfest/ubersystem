What You Get For Volunteering

The more hours you work, the more you get (don't forget about weighted hours):
  • Working at least 6 hours gets you a {{ EVENT_NAME }} t-shirt.

  • Working at least 12 hours gets you food in addition to the shirt. We have professional chefs preparing 3 meals per day throughout the event.

  • Working at least 18 hours gets you a complementary Staff badge for next year's {{ EVENT_NAME }}, which will make you eligible for space in one of our staff hotel rools.

  • Working at least 24 hours gets you a refund on your badge for this year.

  • Working at least 30 hours ONLY IF you're a returning Staffer gets you space in one of our staff hotel rooms.
