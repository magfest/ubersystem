Use this form to remove and add custom purchases and credits or non-Stripe payments and refunds for this {{ model_str }}. You will not be able to remove purchases or credits that were created before a sucessful payment was made. Confirmed Stripe payments or refunds that have a Stripe ID will also be locked.

Enter a positive number in the amount field to add a purchase, and a negative number to apply a credit. Similarly, payments and refunds can be tracked using positive and negative amounts, respectively.

If a payment was made and confirmed through Stripe, you can refund part or all of it using the "Refund Transaction" button. Note that some Stripe payments may appear on multiple receipts, for example, when somone registers multiple badges in one transaction.

Please always itemize purchases and credits. Items with the same price, and only items with the same price, may be grouped into one purchase by setting the Amount as the per-item price and the Quantity field to how many items were purchased.
