Hotel Signups

Below is the reservations link to access the pool of Gaylord hotel rooms held out for our participants. You can book ONE room per studio.

When you click on that link, you will be taken to a landing page where you enter your check-in and check-out dates, and you can select the number of occupants in your room. All of these rooms are double-queen bed rooms (we know it doesn't look like that - they are!) Please reach out to {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_EMAIL|email_only|email_to_link }} if you need an additional night that isn't showing as available.

NOTE FOR THIS YEAR: This system only allows a max 3 persons to be entered as occupants. If you have a 4th person, please add their name to the Requests box below where you are able to list the other occupants, and they can be added to your room by the Gaylord later.

If you have any questions while making your reservation, please reach out to {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_EMAIL|email_only|email_to_link }}.
