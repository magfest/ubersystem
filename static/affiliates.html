Affiliates

We like to know who our Supporters are and what other gaming communities they strongly identify with. So we allow you to choose a group, such as OCRemix or ScrewAttack, so we'll know which of these groups' fans are strongly supporting us.

If we see a lot of Supporters identifying with a specific group, we can put the money from those registrations into things which that group will find important. We might also find some extra-special swag that will be meaningful to that group.

You can choose one of the default affiliate groups, enter a new one, or ignore this completely.
